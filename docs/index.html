Conclusions

1.Of the most film types, there are always one or two special ones that are especially popular among people, such as the sci-fi Inception, The Shawshank Redemption of the criminal class, Farewell My Concubine or Forrest Gump of the love story class and 3 idiots, these high-rating movies can be recommended to everyone, which we’ve already know.

2.Everyone loves happy ending movies - this is the common phenomenon of the Chinese audience. We all like a big reunion at the ending.

3.The community that likes science books especially likes suspense.

4.Those who read about Literature, philosophy and popular type prefer movies that have an insight into the philosophy of life, even if those movies are niche, they also like classic movies, no matter the type. And the Science group is stricter, they just look at the types that you like.

5.People who prefer popular don't like stimulating movies, but they like movies with a tight narrative, such as Black Swan, Léon, You Are the Apple of My Eye.

6.The literary community is fond of the plot, no matter the movies’ popularity, but also likes the family movie.

7.Communities that prefer culture and philosophy, as well as the one prefer popularity, have a great preference for comedy.

8.Those who reads philosophy and the economy like science fiction movies very much.

Recommendations

1. Like economics books: recommend sci-fi movies, such as The Butterfly Effect, "2012", Inception, we can also recommend movies of the compact plot or suspense type

2. Like popular literature books: Recommend movies that contain certain life philosophies, never recommend horror or thriller, recommend classic old movies, and some family movies.

3. Like culture and books with some philosophical elements: similar to popular literature, but they prefer comedy

4. Like science books: Recommendations should be careful, these people are strict, mainly recommend suspense, science fiction, stimulating plots, if it does not work ,we can also recommend comedy for insurance.

5. Like philosophy: the same strict category, but they really like movies with philosophical insights and dramatic twists and turns, as well as science fiction movies.

6. Like literature: Basically saying, they can watch everything. But slightly less like science fiction movies.

7. Almost everyone will like high-scoring movies, as well as comedy movies, and some plots full of life philosophy will be welcomed among most people
